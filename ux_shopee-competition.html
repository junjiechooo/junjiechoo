Shopee Product & Design Challenge Competition 2020

Open to all undergraduates, postgraduates and professionals in Singapore, our case competition is specially designed by the Shopee Product & Design teams. With a focus on Product Management & UI / UX Design, we are looking for talented Product & Design Professionals and Students to share your ideas on how we can create the ultimate feature for the Shopee app.

Shopee believes that online shopping should be accessible, easy and enjoyable — Make the difference with us through Product & Design, and push the boundaries of E-Commerce.


Theme

Engaging the 2021 Local* Consumer

* Participants can choose to base on any of the 7 markets that Shopee is based in: Singapore, Indonesia, Thailand, Vietnam, Malaysia, Philippines, and Taiwan.


Problem Statement

With 90% of people in Southeast Asia and Taiwan connecting to the internet mainly via mobile, many features and initiatives on Shopee are designed for mobile. Taking into consideration Shopee’s current initiatives on its mobile app in the respectives markets and local consumer behaviour, propose a solution that focuses on

  1. Improving an existing feature or
  2. Creating a new feature that would enable Shopee to remain the leading e-commerce platform in your chosen market.

Shopee believes that online shopping should be accessible, easy and enjoyable — Make the difference with us through Product & Design, and push the boundaries of E-Commerce.

Areas that your solution can target, but are not limited to:

Listings, Search, Recommendation, Market, Supply Chain, Financial Services, Anti-Fraud, Notification Channels

Product Specification

These new features aim to implement it gradually as it would require time for the tech department to model products from shopee sellers who wish to increase conversions and decrease the return rate of buyers . Using 3D modelling of the product will allow users to be able to have a feel and look at how the product will look like on them before purchasing it. Another way to get product modelled will be to engage with 3D product model company such as Style.me, NBYIT.

In addition, with the avatar details such as height, weight, and size of the body, there will be an automatic recommendation for sizing which will aid users in selecting the size that will mostly suit them. The recommendation of the size is purely based on the history of buyers with the same avatar details. For instance, for a clothes of size M fits users with the avatar weight ranging from 60 - 65 kilograms, height ranging from 170 - 175 centimeters, average body size and average body shape. This feature was further improved from ASOS fit assistant 4 .

Lastly, we also incorporate the idea of having Augmented Reality cameras for users to place virtual objects in any specific area they wish to see how the product can fit in the space. We also implemented screenshot capability for users to share or save the image compare it with other alternative products they intend to purchase. This allows users to be able to plan their purchase well as the size of the product is exactly the same.

Business Model

What Shopee does: Marketplace for regional sellers and buyers

As the target audience is the layman, it would be helpful having more visual elements rather than specifications. Taking clothings for example, having models wearing the clothes, alongside clothing specification, is insufficient for consumers to visualise themselves in the same clothes. Similarly, furniture are larger items around the house, making it difficult to visualise how the new furniture could complement the existing ones. Noting the competitive landscape, AR technology is not widely used in the market currently.

Although some applications have this feature already, such as IKEA Place, by implementing the use of AR, this allows Shopee to be one step ahead of their competitors as consumers would feel more secure about how they look like in the clothes of their choice, or how the furniture would fit in their houses. With a further customization of an avatar, this allows consumers to feel more connected and that the app is personalised.

By adding the AR feature with a personalised touch, this would help Shopee become a one-stop solution , increasing customer retention rate and help Shopee grow and stay relevant.

Learnings & Future Plans

I learned that I should also consider the feasibility of the project based on the company current ability and not to go too far stretch. I should also take into account of their company's finances so that they would not need to incur too much cost.
